Discover

As a part of Discover's internal marketing design team I initiated a project to redesign the homepage of Discover. The redesign was presented to the CMO of the company and launched a full scale initiative to modernize the online public face of Discover.

The goal was not only to provide a simple user experience that would allow existing card holders to easily log in to their accounts, but also provide an enhanced tool for marketing to potential customers.
