Website Application

Cookmore by Kenmore

In 2011 our team was tasked with creating a recipe site to be tied in with Kenmore brand appliances. It needed to accept user generated recipes, brand supplied recipes, allow for creation of 'cookbooks' (both online and printed) and be responsive to accommodate the full range of mobile devices.

Our solution was Cookmore.com. On the project I created initial wireframes and user flows, visual/UI design and worked with the development team to do some of the front-end coding as well. I continued to support the project for 3 years adding features such as a menu planner and nutrition facts for recipes.
