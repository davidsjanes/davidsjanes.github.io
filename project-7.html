Website Redesign

Amana

In 2014 Amana appliance brand wanted to redesign their site to make it easier to browse product and tie into their eco-focused campaign. Our solution was to make the product browsing a single page experience with the only secondary pages being product details. For this project I created many interactive prototypes and general UI design for product browsing and product details.

The site has since gone through a visual redesign to move away from the eco-focus, but it has retained the spirit of my initial UI.
