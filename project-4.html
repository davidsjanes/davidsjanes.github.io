Homepage Redesign

Craftsman

Every holiday season (Christmas and Black Friday) Craftsman updates their homepage to be more promotional in order to sell more product. For the 2013 holiday season I was tasked with making this update. I really wanted to create a clean, well branded homepage that would not only promote product but promote Craftsman as a brand.

This design was meant to be temporary, but due to the pages' success it was made the basis for future iterations of their homepage going forward. The site has recently undergone a refresh so the link is to my local development version.
