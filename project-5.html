Social Application

Craftsman Club Workshop

In 2014 Sears wanted to create an application for their Shop Your Way e-commerce platform that would allow users to manage their tool collections, explore new tools and share projects with one another. I was contracted from my agency to work in-house with their team for a 3 month stint to guide the front end design, UI and feature set.

It was an intense process with a lot of stakeholders (including the CEO), but in the end we had a product that all stakeholders were happy with and still exists as one of the few apps remaining on the platform today. The live site is gated by a login so the link is to my internal prototype.
